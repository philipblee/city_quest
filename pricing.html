Pricing Questions & Answers

Everything you need to know about CityQuest pricing

Can I switch plans anytime?

Yes! You can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. Upgrades take effect immediately, while downgrades take effect at your next billing cycle. Your collectible cards and progress are always preserved.

What happens if I cancel my subscription?

You'll keep access to premium features until your current billing period ends, then revert to the Free Explorer plan. Your photos, achievements, and cards remain safe - you just won't be able to collect new cards beyond the 5-slot limit.

How does the points system work exactly?

Points are awarded automatically when you complete activities. They never expire and can be redeemed for premium subscription time or physical rewards. Points earned through referrals are credited when your friend completes their first quest.

Are there family or group discounts?

Yes! Families of 4+ get 25% off annual plans. Student discounts (50% off) are available with valid .edu email. Corporate and group rates start at 10+ participants.

Which cities are included?

Currently launching in Boston with plans for NYC, San Francisco, Washington DC, Chicago, LA, Seattle, and Philadelphia. City access depends on your plan level - check the comparison above for details.

Is there a free trial for premium plans?

The Free Explorer plan lets you experience core features with no time limit. We also offer a 7-day free trial of Quest Master for new users who complete their first quest within 48 hours of signing up.
