About This Tour

Step into America's first public garden, where literary classics come to life and Victorian elegance meets modern Boston. This carefully crafted 10-stop journey takes you through the most significant landmarks of the Boston Public Garden, from the beloved Make Way for Ducklings statues to the historic Swan Boats.

You'll discover the garden's role in children's literature, its innovative landscape design, and its importance as Boston's green heart. Each stop includes photo verification, historical context, and the opportunity to chat with AI historical figures who shaped this remarkable space.

What You'll Experience

  • Literary Legacy: Explore locations from beloved children's books
  • Victorian Design: Understand the garden's innovative landscape architecture
  • Swan Boat History: Learn about this unique Boston tradition
  • Botanical Beauty: Discover rare plants and seasonal displays
  • Public Art: Appreciate sculptures and memorials throughout the garden
  • AI Conversations: Chat with historical figures about the garden's evolution
