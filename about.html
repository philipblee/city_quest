From Corporate Success to Educational Innovation

After leading a 500-person technology company to $150M in revenue, I realized that the most meaningful experiences came not from boardrooms, but from walking Boston's historic streets and sharing stories with fellow history enthusiasts.

As a Boston Latin School graduate, I grew up surrounded by the very history that shaped America. Yet I watched countless tourists rush past profound historical sites without truly understanding their significance. Meanwhile, local residents often overlooked the remarkable stories happening right in their neighborhoods.

CityQuest was born from a simple belief: history should be personal, accessible, and fun. By combining AI technology with proven gamification psychology, we're creating the first platform that makes cultural education as engaging as entertainment.
