How CityQuest Works

Explore cities through interactive tours, AI conversations, and collectible gaming

Your Urban Adventure in 4 Simple Steps

From download to discovery, here's how CityQuest transforms city exploration

1

Choose Your Quest

Select from multiple walking tours in your city. Each quest features 10-20 historic locations with fascinating stories, from the Freedom Trail in Boston to Central Park in NYC.

  • Multiple difficulty levels
  • Estimated time and distance
  • Historical themes and topics
  • Accessibility information
2

Navigate to Locations

Follow GPS directions to each historic landmark. Our smart routing optimizes your path and provides context about what you'll discover at each stop.

  • Turn-by-turn GPS navigation
  • Offline maps available
  • Real-time location verification
  • Safety and accessibility notes
3

Take Photos & Learn

Capture photos at each location to verify your visit. Discover rich historical content, chat with AI historical figures, and unlock collectible cards.

  • Photo verification system
  • AI historical conversations
  • Interactive multimedia content
  • Collectible card discoveries
4

Collect & Share

Build your collection of People, Places, Events, and Things cards. Share achievements, compete with friends, and unlock special rewards.

  • Four collectible categories
  • Rarity system with special cards
  • Social sharing and competition
  • Achievement badges and certificates

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about getting started

Do I need to download an app?

Currently, CityQuest works directly in your web browser on any device. No app store downloads required! We're also developing a native mobile app for an even better experience.

What if I don't have reliable internet during my tour?

Tours work offline once started! We cache all location data, historical content, and maps locally. You only need internet to begin the tour and upload your final photos.

How accurate is the historical information?

All content is fact-checked by professional historians and cross-referenced with academic sources. Our AI conversations are designed to be educational and historically accurate.

Can I do tours with friends and family?

Absolutely! CityQuest supports group tours, family challenges, and team-building experiences. Everyone can participate on their own device while sharing the adventure.

What happens to my photos and data?

Your photos are stored securely and privately. You control sharing settings and can delete your data anytime. We use photos only for tour verification and your personal collection.
