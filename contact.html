Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

How do I get beta access?

Simply fill out the beta form above! We'll send you early access within 24-48 hours along with instructions for getting started with the Boston Public Garden tour.

Is the beta completely free?

Yes! Beta testers get full access to all premium features while we're in development. This includes AI conversations, unlimited quests, and collectible cards.

What cities will be available at launch?

We're starting with Boston and planning to expand to NYC, San Francisco, Washington DC, Chicago, LA, Seattle, and Philadelphia based on beta feedback and demand.

Can I suggest content or locations?

Absolutely! Beta testers are encouraged to suggest new locations, historical content corrections, and feature ideas. Your feedback directly shapes the platform's development.

How do corporate partnerships work?

We offer custom team-building experiences ($25-50/participant), educational institution licensing, and tourism board partnerships ($50K-200K). Contact us for a tailored proposal.

When will the full platform launch?

We're planning a public launch in Q2 2025, with the Boston area fully featured. Additional cities will roll out throughout 2025 based on beta testing results and community demand.
